How it works

Clear rules, consistent pickup, and a clean community in three steps.

1

Residents set out

Residents place tied bags in approved containers outside their doors during the set-out window.

2

Attendant collects

Uniformed attendants collect bags, keep hallways clear, and follow the nightly checklist.

3

Trash is disposed

We dispose at the compactor or dumpster and document exceptions with notes or photos for your team.
